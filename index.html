Home

Existence
In the pursuit of these questions, we discover the tapestry of life and the inexhaustible wellspring of what it truly means to live.

On Poetry

The essence of poetry lies in its ability to use words creatively, employing sound, rhythm, and language to evoke feelings and provoke thought. It often captures the beauty of language and explores the complexities of human experience in a condensed and imaginative form. Poets use their words not only to convey literal meaning but also to create a deeper emotional and intellectual impact on the reader or listener. Poetry can cover a wide range of themes, from love and nature to social and political commentary, making it a versatile and diverse art form.
